E-visa online

Apply online at evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn (official only). Takes 3 working days, costs ~£20. Valid up to 90 days, single or multiple entry.

∼£20 · Official site only
📄

Visa on arrival

Requires a pre-approval letter and cash payment at the airport. Available for air travel only. Less convenient than the e-visa.

Air entry only

How to apply for a Vietnam e-visa — step by step
1

Use the official government site only

Go to evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn — avoid any third-party sites that charge extra fees for the exact same process.

2

Upload your documents

You need a passport-style photo on a white background, plus a clear scan of your passport bio page. Have your travel dates and intended border crossing ready before you start.

3

Pay approximately £20 by card

Payment is made online by credit or debit card. Save your application reference number — you will need it to check the status of your application.

4

Wait 3 working days, then print it out

Download your approved e-visa as a PDF and print it. You must present the printed e-visa alongside your physical passport at immigration on arrival.
